OVA (Original Video Animation) is anime that is released on DVD and Blu-ray rather than being broadcast on television. Because they aren't on television they don't have censorhip. They usually add additional content or alternative storylines for existing series.

ONA (Original Net Animation) is anime that is first released on online streaming platform like Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, etc. They usually are bonus side-stories or short promotional content for already existing series. But lately there are more and more full anime series that are ONA, like Cyberpunk: Edgerunners and JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Stone Ocean.
